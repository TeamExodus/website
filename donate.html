About Donations

This distribution was made possible by contributions to Team Exodus from users like you. Thank you.

    All donations contribute to one of the following...
  • Funding build/web server used to make nightly builds, host downloads, and web pages including this one
  • Supporting the developers who spend hours each day coding for Exodus Android.
  • Adding support to more devices**

**Team Exodus can only provide support for devices that a team member has the ability to test on. In order to add support for new devices a team member has to obtain the device to ensure the device is fully compatible with Exodus Android.
